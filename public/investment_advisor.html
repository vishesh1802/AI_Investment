Labeling Strategy
◆ BUY
Forward 60-day return > sector 60th percentile AND volatility < sector median AND risk score < 40
◆ HOLD
Neither qualifying as BUY nor AVOID. Mixed signals or insufficient momentum. Confidence below 65% triggers ABSTAIN override.
◆ AVOID
Forward return < sector 30th percentile OR volatility > 90th pct OR risk score > 70
⚠ Labels are derived from historical data using time-aware splits. No forward-looking data leaks into features. All temporal features use rolling windows anchored strictly before the label period.
